# Brett Moore — Client Profile

Source: [Kickoff Brief](https://www.notion.so/3104d34703628156ab96f5e23700217d)

## Who He Is
Australian entrepreneur based in Barcelona. Law/business/tech background, 6 startups (mostly MedTech). Lifelong ambition: journalism and interviewing. Political and science junkie since age 11. Reads 2+ newspapers daily, obsessive online reader. 17 years in the US, plugged into international news. Working with startups so can't dedicate 5 days/week to this — passion project, not a business.

## What He Wants
"I read something interesting this morning" → "it's live on five platforms with clips" in under an hour of his time.

## Content Vision
Three shows:
- **Politics** — Current affairs, geopolitics. Brett hosts. Synthesis + analysis with personal overlay.
- **Science** — Evolution, deep ocean, animal behavior, new substances. More curiosity, less opinion.
- **Quirky** — Human interest stories. Hardest to automate. Serendipitous stories.

**Format:** Multi-topic, 5-7 min, 3 topics with section markers. Each section = standalone clip.
**Schedule:** Quality-driven, not fixed. Some weeks 3 episodes, some weeks 1.
**Distribution:** "Everything under the sun. Wide as possible." YouTube, Shorts, Threads, all platforms.

## News Sources (confirmed)
NYT, LA Times, Financial Times, Vox, Atlantic, Vanity Fair, Foreign Policy, Scientific American. ~14 outlets total. Has active subscriptions. Will send curated list.

## #1 Concern: Accuracy & Trust
Said twice in meeting: "Extremely important that information is accurate and truthful, does not infringe copyright, complies with publication laws." He red-teams everything 2-3 times. Has 7 pages of legal guidelines he feeds into AI systems.

## Target Audience
"People who read a lot and think a lot about things." Mature, intellectually curious, bored by cable news. His friend group "The Who's" (~10 people) as early audience: filmmaker, photographer, academic, architect. Smart, culturally engaged, hate bad reporting.

## Communication Style
WhatsApp preferred. On it all day, fine with voice messages. Two groups (one with team, one with mentor).

## Voice & Tone (for persona transform)
- Informed but approachable
- Strong opinions backed by sources
- Australian wit, conversational
- Not academic or preachy
- Synthesis + personal overlay, not just reporting
