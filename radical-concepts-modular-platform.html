Learning + Intelligence

Source Ingestion

928 RSS feeds curated by Hamid across politics, science, and human interest. Jina Reader API extracts full article text as clean markdown. n8n on Oracle Cloud (free tier) orchestrates the schedule — fresh content every morning. Brett can also submit URLs manually. The Source Intelligence module validates, deduplicates, and detects emerging trends before anything reaches the filter.

Filter & Persona Scoring

Two modules working in concert. Source Intelligence clusters and deduplicates across sources. The Persona Engine scores each article for "Brett-worthiness" — relevance, novelty, and alignment with his editorial taste. The persona profile encodes his vocabulary, hot takes, analysis style, and topic preferences. Together they produce a ranked, themed feed — not a flat list.

Brett's Editorial Gate

Nothing enters production without Brett's explicit approval. Two human gates exist: topic selection (which stories to pursue) and final production approval (script and media sign-off). Whether this surfaces as a dashboard or WhatsApp is an open architectural question. This is where his 7 pages of legal guidelines get enforced. Red-team everything 2–3 times — accuracy and trust are his number one concern.

Script & Production

The Script & Text Engine handles the fan-out: same source becomes podcast script (C1), quiz questions (C2), newsletter copy (C3), shareable cards. Media Production then takes approved scripts through recording, audio processing, and optionally video assembly. Politics uses Brett's real voice; science and quirky shows may use voice cloning. Configurable per content type.

Multi-Platform Distribution

The Publishing Network handles format adaptation and auto-upload to every platform Brett wants — podcast RSS, YouTube, short-form clips, social posts. Zero manual work after Brett's final approval. Scheduling is configurable: publish immediately or queue for optimal timing.

Engagement & Intelligence

Two modules close the loop. The Learning & Social Engine generates quizzes with spaced repetition, tracks streaks, and produces shareable cards — the differentiator inspired by Duolingo mechanics. The Intelligence & Growth module captures analytics, audience signals, and community interactions, feeding refinements back into Source Intelligence and the Persona Engine. Without this feedback loop, the platform never gets smarter.

  • How to encode Brett's taste? — determines if Persona Engine is a real service or a ghost prompt
